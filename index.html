Welcome

We'd like to welcome you to the Primarily Montessori community; a place for families and educators to find the resources they need for raising and guiding children through the practice of Montessori philosophy.

Join us as we spread our passion for fostering supportive communities and raising confident, happy children.

How do we accomplish this? It starts in our homes, where we create spaces that nourish a child's feelings of belonging and independence. It continues in our classroom environments as we support each aspect of the student's development: physical, social, emotional, academic, and everything in between.

Why is this important? The education of our children comes from understanding how they learn and grow. When we approach their learning as that which it really is - an intrinsic human motivation - we nurture the entirety of the child's development. When we follow the child in this way, we allow them to become.
