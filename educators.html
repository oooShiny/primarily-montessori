Educators

Whether you are new to teaching or experienced in the field, it can helpful to have an experienced teacher as a resource. We offer mentoring and consulting services for educators and schools.

We begin with an initial conversation to hear your needs and understand your goals, and follow up with observations of your classroom and mentor meetings.

Other options include long-term mentoring for Montessori teachers in training, guidance on communication within teaching teams, and help designing prepared environments in Montessori 3-6 classrooms.

Please contact us to specify your needs. We look forward to working with you!
